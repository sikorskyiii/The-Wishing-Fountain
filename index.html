The Wishing Fountain

OSINT challenge · Nuremberg, Germany

In Nuremberg’s main square stands the “Beautiful Fountain,” a 14-meter Gothic work from the 14th century — some say its design was never meant for the square at all.

Hidden in its iron lattice is a small golden ring. Locals whisper:

“Turn it three times while making a wish, and luck may follow.”

Everything you need is in plain sight. Start with the file — and follow the name.
